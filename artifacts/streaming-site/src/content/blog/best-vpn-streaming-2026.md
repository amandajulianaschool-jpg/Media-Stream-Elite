Using a VPN with your streaming service can enhance privacy and help you access content from different regions. Here's our guide to the best VPNs that work seamlessly with StreamVault in 2026.

## Why Use a VPN for Streaming?

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) encrypts your internet traffic and routes it through a server in a location of your choice. For streaming, this offers several benefits:

- **Privacy** — Your ISP cannot see what you're streaming
- **Bypass throttling** — Some ISPs throttle streaming traffic; a VPN prevents this
- **Security** — Encrypted connection protects your data on public Wi-Fi
