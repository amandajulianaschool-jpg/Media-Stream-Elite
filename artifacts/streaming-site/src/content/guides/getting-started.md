# Getting Started with StreamVault

Welcome to StreamVault! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with the ultimate 4K streaming experience.

## Step 1: Choose Your Plan

Visit our pricing page and select the plan that best suits your needs. We recommend the 12-month plan for the best value.

## Step 2: Install the App

Download the StreamVault app from your device's app store or use our web player at streamvault.tv/player.

## Step 3: Start Watching

Enter your credentials and enjoy 20,000+ channels in stunning 4K quality.
